Find the thief

Dave was having lunch in a cafe.He went to the bathroom and forgot his wallet on the table.When he came back,he saw that the wallet was still there,but there was no money in it.
Dave called the police and the policeman arrived and asked,"What happened exactly?" and Dave said that,"Somebody has stolen money from my wallet while I was in the bathroom".The policeman checked the CCTV footage immediately and he saw three people(Alice the waiter, Sarah the cleaner and Stan the visitor) around the table. He questioned them individually,Alice said,"I just came to grab the empty glass and I didnt touch the wallet", Sarah said,"I wiped the floor under the table and I left" and Stan said,"I just passed by".The policeman pulled the fingerprints from the wallet and there were only dave's fingerprints on it.

So Who is the Thief?
